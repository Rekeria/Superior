Warrior has 3 subclasses: berserker, knight, and magic swordsman

Berserker:
The berserker is categorized by 2 traits: disregard for self protection and unquenchable blood lust.
However, the berserker does not necessarily have no survivability, each sacrifices long term survivability for short term immortality
2 specializations: pact fighter, True berserker, Thrall(hidden growth)
The pact fighter is a powerful ancestral warrior, who harnesses the souls of his ancestors to empower him. In exchange for power, they are also driven mad.
