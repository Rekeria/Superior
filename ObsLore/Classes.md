There are 5 primary classes: Mage, Warrior, Archer, Rogue, and Enabler. Every player is only allowed to choose one class and cannot "multi class". Classes possess subclasses, and specializations within the subclasses which form subsubclasses, aka specializations.

[[Mage]]:
Mages are the epitome of glass cannons. Mages tend to be highly immobile casters that focus on performing long incantations. The subclasses are: elementalist, spiritualist, arcanist.


[[Archer]]:
Archers are ranged foes who decimate their foes from a distance. The subclasses are: sharpshooter, hunter, and The hwacha.


[[Rogue]]:
Rogues are a misc class that focuses on high mobility.
The subclasses are: Assassin, battle dancer, and (placeholder)

[[Warrior]]:
Warriors are powerful melee warriors who utilize sustainability to hold the fore line in battle.
The subclasses are: berserker, knight, and magic swordsman.
